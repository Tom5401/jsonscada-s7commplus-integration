2. Apply display filter: `s7comm-plus` (or capture filter on PLC IP)
3. In TIA Portal Online & Diagnostics, navigate to the alarm view showing active unacknowledged alarms

5. Stop the Wireshark capture
6. Find the `SetVariableRequest` PDU sent from TIA Portal to the PLC immediately after the ack click

   - `Address` value (the LID/attribute ID for the ack operation)
   - `Value` type and encoding (what carries the cpuAlarmId — likely ValueLWord or similar)
   - `TransportFlags` value (0x34 for response-required, or 0x74 for no-response)
8. Also note if a `SetVariableResponse` PDU comes back from the PLC (confirms response-required path)

Provide Claude with:
- The decoded field values (InObjectId, Address, Value type/encoding, TransportFlags)
- The raw hex bytes of the SetVariableRequest PDU payload (optional but helpful)
- The cpuAlarmId of the alarm that was acknowledged (for correlation)
- Whether a SetVariableResponse was received
